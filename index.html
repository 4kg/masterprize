MASTERPRIZE

MASTERPRIZE is a Go (Baduk) tournament concept focused on real monetary motivation.

The core idea is simple: prize money should be accessible not only to top players, but to anyone willing to play and improve.

Concept

  • Cash-prize Go tournaments
  • Handicap / MacMahon-based formats
  • Fair competition across skill levels
  • Focus on participation, not elite exclusivity

Why MASTERPRIZE

Traditional Go tournaments often reward only the strongest players. MASTERPRIZE is designed to keep players engaged by making the reward structure meaningful for a much wider audience.

Status

Project in early development.
Concept testing and format refinement in progress.
